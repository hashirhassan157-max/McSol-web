Our Premium Services

Comprehensive digital solutions powered by cutting-edge fiber optic technology

Point-to-Point Optical Fiber Connectivity

Dark Core Connectivity Over Optical Fiber

Specializes in networking architecture using dark fiber strands for high-speed, dedicated communication links, and leases unlit fiber throughout Faisalabad and its suburbs.

Data Centre and Co-Location Services

Features a state-of-the-art Data Centre and Server Room in Faisalabad, providing infrastructure hosting, managed services, connectivity services, environmental control, and uninterrupted power with multi-layer security.

CDN Services

We fulfill bandwidth requirements, whether raw or mixed with CDNs, dealing in both asymmetric and symmetric bandwidths. Wherever you need it in Pakistan, we've got you covered.

FTTH (Fiber to the Home) GPON Services

Offers high-speed broadband services with unlimited data packages and unparalleled internet speeds at affordable rates in Faisalabad and its suburbs, supported by Transworld.
